Legal & Safety Notes

Protective Mechanisms & Default Risks›
  • Escrow Accounts: For off-plan, your payments must go into a DLD-regulated Escrow account, not the developer's personal account. This ensures funds are used for construction.
  • Oqood: The pre-registration title for off-plan properties. Ensure you receive this certificate.
  • Default Consequences: If you fail to pay off-plan installments, the developer may retain 25-40% of funds paid depending on construction progress (check your SPA).
  • Housing Fee (Municipality): 5% of annual rent is typically paid by the tenant via DEWA bills (for contracts >1yr).
