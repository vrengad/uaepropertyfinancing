Key UAE Fees & Financing Rules

Checklist of mandatory charges, upfront payments, running costs, and exit fees.

1) Acquisition Costs

Secondary vs Off-plan

  • DLD fee: 4% of purchase price for both secondary and off-plan.
  • Developer admin: ~55k AED (~$15k) only for off-plan (set as the default admin fee).
  • Agency commission: 2% on secondary. Off-plan: 0% when buying direct
  • Trustee & legal: ~18k AED (~$5k) on secondary; usually N/A for off-plan.

2) Upfront Payment Rules

Residency and property type matter

  • Minimum ticket: ~550k AED (~$150k) to enter the market.
  • Down payment (secondary): 20% for residents; 40% for non-residents (auto-applied when you flip residency).
  • Off-plan entry: optional EOI from ~26k AED (~$7k) plus a mandatory 20% down payment (includes 10% booking).
  • Developer plans: interest-free 50/50 or 70/30 schedules tied to build milestones. Price premium of 5–15% vs cash.
  • Mortgages: not available for off-plan. Non-resident secondary mortgages exist but carry higher rates and lower approval odds.

3) Post-Purchase Costs

Recurring charges impacting cashflow

  • Service charges: typically $3–6 (≈11–22 AED) per sq ft per year. 1BHK (800 sq ft) ≈ 2.5k–4k USD.
  • Housing fee (5% of annual rent): usually paid by the tenant on long-term contracts via DEWA.
  • Property management: around 20% for short-term (Airbnb) and 5–8% for long-term.

4) Exit Costs

Selling a property

  • Agency commission: negotiated with broker (default 2% in the model).
  • NOC: required from developer; typically up to $1k (~3.7k AED).

Remember: IRR assumes constant FX and excludes income taxes.

Legal & Safety Notes

Protective Mechanisms & Default Risks›
  • Escrow Accounts: For off-plan, your payments must go into a DLD-regulated Escrow account, not the developer's personal account. This ensures funds are used for construction.
  • Bank Guarantees: Registered developers typically post performance guarantees to protect buyers if a project stalls or is cancelled.
  • Oqood: The pre-registration title for off-plan properties. Ensure you receive this certificate.
  • Default Consequences: If you fail to pay off-plan installments, the developer may retain 25-40% of funds paid depending on construction progress (check your SPA).
  • Housing Fee (Municipality): 5% of annual rent is typically paid by the tenant via DEWA bills (for contracts >1yr).
